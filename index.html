Théo Gnassounou

theo.gnassounou@epfl.ch.

I am currently a Post-Doc researcher at EPFL in the ECEO team under the supervision of Devis Tuia. I completed my PhD at Inria Saclay in the Mind team under the supervision of Rémi Flamary and Alexandre Gramfort.

My research interests include machine learning, domain adaptation, optimal transport and their applications to time series and remote sensing.

I am also interested in open science and reproducible research. I am the co-creator and maintainer of Skada, a Python library for domain adaptation.
